Hi there!

I’m Kassidy Tran, a digital product designer based in Orange County, CA. I recently graduated from the UCI UX/UI Design Boot Camp, but before that, I studied Biology at UCLA on a pre-med career track. I love problem solving and exploring solutions creatively, so I’m transitioning from a career in health sciences to a career in tech and design.

I approach design with the intent to create simplified and intuitive products, and solve actual user problems. Because of this, I focus on the research and testing phases of the design process. To me, design is like a puzzle on how to strike a balance between usability, efficiency, and profitability.

When I'm not designing, I love to crochet, play video games, and spoil my dog Peanut with park outings and pup-cups!
